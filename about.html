ABOUT PORTFOLIO

about the designer

I was the child who was known for being "good at drawing". I have been asked to draw princesses, portraits of crushes, and even flaming basketball hoops. When I had to pick a major in college, I didn't think I could make a living on just being "good at drawing" so I decided a career path in graphic design seemed like a reasonable alternative to "starving artist".

I currently reside near Madison, WI. I have a wonderful husband who keeps me on my toes and a sassy little girl who keeps me laughing. I spend about 10-20 hours a week reading pretty much any book or article I can get my hands on.

P.S. My last name is pronounced BRUNN-GRAY-BURR.
